Our Story

A Highway Landmark
on NH-19

Raj Hotel & Restaurant is a beloved highway food stop on NH-19 in Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh — known for delicious North Indian food, Chinese dishes, fresh sizzlers and traditional sweets.

Travelers and families enjoy quick service, hot meals and a comfortable dining environment. Whether you're passing through or looking for a satisfying meal, Raj Hotel is your perfect stop.
